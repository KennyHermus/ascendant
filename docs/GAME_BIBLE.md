# Ascendant Game Bible

Version: 0.1

---

# 1. Game Identity

## Name

Ascendant

## Theme

A hero begins weak, inexperienced, and limited.

Through discipline, effort, learning, and perseverance, they ascend beyond their previous limits.

The player's real-world actions determine the hero's growth.

---

# 2. Core Fantasy

The player should feel:

"I am becoming stronger."

Not only inside the game.

In reality.

---

# 3. Player Role

The player controls a hero on the Path of Resolve.

The hero represents:

- Physical capability
- Mental capability
- Discipline
- Knowledge
- Character

The hero begins at:

Level 1

All stats:

1

The journey starts from weakness.

# 5. Hero Attributes

The hero has eight core attributes.

---



## Strength

Represents:

- Physical power
- Muscle development
- Force generation

Improved by:

- Strength training
- Physical challenges
- Athletic achievements

---



## HP

Represents:

- Overall health
- Recovery ability
- Resilience

Improved by:

- Sleep consistency
- Nutrition
- Health habits

---



## Defense

Represents:

- Ability to resist setbacks
- Stability
- Protection against failure

Improved by:

- Discipline
- Preparation
- Organization

---



## Stamina

Represents:

- Endurance
- Energy
- Long-term effort

Improved by:

- Cardio
- Consistent work
- Extended challenges

---



## Speed

Represents:

- Efficiency
- Reaction ability
- Execution speed

Improved by:

- Time management
- Quick execution
- Practice

---



## Intellect

Represents:

- Knowledge
- Problem solving
- Learning

Improved by:

- Reading
- Studying
- Courses
- Skill development

---



## Willpower

Represents:

- Discipline
- Self-control
- Mental toughness

Improved by:

- Completing difficult tasks
- Avoiding distractions
- Maintaining streaks

---



## Special Technique

Represents:

Unique abilities unlocked through progression.

Unlike other stats, this is purely game-based.

Examples:

- Focus Mode
- Second Wind
- Limit Break
- Perfect Execution

---



# 6. Game Philosophy

The game should reward:

Consistency > Intensity

A person completing small tasks every day should eventually surpass someone who acts occasionally.

---



# 7. Failure Philosophy

Failure is part of progression.

Missing quests should not destroy progress.

Failure creates opportunities:

- Recovery quests
- Reflection
- Comeback bonuses

The goal is returning stronger.
