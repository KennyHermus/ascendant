# Combat System

> **Status: Future — v0.1.x only.** Not implemented. Do not build combat in v0.0.x milestones.

Version: 0.1 (design)

---

# Purpose

Combat transforms personal growth into visible challenges.

The player does not fight because they clicked enough buttons.

The player fights because their real-world growth created a stronger hero.

---

# Combat Philosophy

Combat should represent:

- Preparation

- Progress

- Strategy

- Growth

The goal is not grinding.

The goal is overcoming meaningful challenges.

---

# Combat Structure

A battle consists of:

Player Stats

Equipment

Abilities

Enemy Stats

Combat Calculation

Outcome

Rewards



---

# Enemy Types

## Normal Enemies

Encountered regularly.

Purpose:

- Test progress

- Provide rewards

- Introduce mechanics

Examples:

- Training Dummy

- Shadow Beast

- Lost Wanderer

- Lesser Guardian

---

## Elite Enemies

Stronger versions of normal enemies.

Appear:

Every 5 levels

Rewards:

- Better equipment

- Increased currency

- Unique items

---

## Boss Enemies

Major progression milestones.

Appear:

Every 10 levels

Boss fights should feel like:

"The moment I finally became strong enough."

---

# Enemy Scaling

Enemies scale based on:

- Hero level

- Previous victories

- Current progression

Avoid infinite grinding.

A strong player should feel strong.

# Victory Rewards

Winning grants:

- XP

- Currency

- Items

- Equipment

- Story progression

---

# Defeat System

Failure is not a game over.

Instead:

The player receives:

- Recovery quests

- Reflection objectives

- Temporary challenges

Example:

Enemy:

"The Iron Guardian"

Defeat condition:

Player lacks stamina.

Recovery quests:

- Complete 3 cardio sessions

- Maintain sleep schedule

- Complete hydration goals

After completion:

Rematch available.
