# Story Identity

Ascendant follows the journey of a hero discovering their potential.

The story mirrors the player's own development.

---

# Setting

The world contains individuals who possess hidden potential.

Most people remain dormant.

A rare few awaken their ability to grow beyond normal limits.

These individuals are known as:

"The Ascended."

---

# The Player

The player begins as an unawakened individual.

They possess:

- Minimal power

- No reputation

- No abilities

Their only advantage:

The willingness to improve.

---

# The Path of Resolve

The hero discovers that power is created through:

- Discipline

- Learning

- Persistence

- Self-mastery

The Path of Resolve is not about defeating others.

It is about defeating the weaker version of yourself.

---

# Story Structure

## Chapter 1: Awakening

Theme:

Beginning the journey.

Features unlocked:

- Quests

- Stats

- Basic progression

---

## Chapter 2: Foundation

Theme:

Building consistency.

Features:

- Equipment

- Basic enemies

- First challenges

---

## Chapter 3: Ascension

Theme:

Breaking previous limits.

Features:

- Advanced abilities

- Boss battles

- Major transformations

---

# Transformations

Inspired by training arcs.

Transformations are earned.

Not given.

Examples:

## Focus State

Requirement:

Strong consistency streak.

Effect:

Improved learning rewards.

---

## Limit Break

Requirement:

Overcome a major challenge.

Effect:

Temporary stat boost.

---

## Ascendant Form

Ultimate progression state.

Requirement:

Long-term mastery.

---

# Story Philosophy

The player's real journey is the story.

The game should never feel separate from life.
